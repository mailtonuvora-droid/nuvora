Why Choose Sprouted Health Mix?

Sprouting is a natural process that transforms seeds into tiny, nutrient-packed powerhouses. At Nuvora, we use this traditional method to ensure you get the maximum possible nutrition from every spoonful.

Higher Nutrient Density

Sprouting increases levels of vitamins (C, B, and E) and minerals (Calcium, Iron, Zinc). It breaks down anti-nutrients like phytic acid, making these minerals easier for your body to absorb.

Easy Digestion

The sprouting process begins the breakdown of complex starches and proteins into simpler forms. This makes it extremely gentle on the stomach, making it ideal for babies and seniors.

The Nuvora Sprouting Secret

Our Sprouted Healthy Mix combines Sprouted Nuts, Sprouted Millets, and Microgreen Powder to create a balanced superfood. Each ingredient is carefully selected:

  • Sprouted Almonds & Walnuts: Essential fats and brain-boosting nutrients.
  • Sprouted Ragi & Bajra: High calcium and fiber for bone health.
  • Microgreen Powder: Incredible antioxidant boost in every serve.

How it Helps with Weight Gain

Unlike artificial energy drinks, Sprouted Health Mix provides clean, calorie-dense nutrition that helps in healthy weight gain by building muscle and bone mass rather than just body fat.
